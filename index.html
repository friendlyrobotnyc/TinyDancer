Tiny Dancer
Made with love by the Robot Flight School Team.
An android library for displaying fps from the choreographer and percentage of time with two or more frames dropped


Last Droidcon Facebook gave a talk about measuring app performance (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xg2CH4aV2Fo). In a similar manner to how Leak Canary brought leak detection to apps at runtime, we wanted to allow apps to see FPS during normal app execution.
Details

To Show the FPS Meter: FPSLibrary.create().show(getApplicationContext()); To Hide the FPS Meter: FPSLibrary.hide((Application) getApplicationContext());
